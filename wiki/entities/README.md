# Entities Directory

Structured pages for companies, products, concepts, and organizations you interact with regularly.

## What Goes Here

- Companies you work with or compete against
- Products you use or evaluate
- Organizations relevant to your work
- Abstract entities that deserve a reference page

## Page Structure

Each entity page should have:
- **Overview**: What it is, why it matters to you
- **Key Facts**: Size, industry, products, people
- **Your Relationship**: How you interact with this entity
- **Recent Developments**: What's changed recently
- **Source**: Where this information came from

## How It Grows

- `/ingest` creates entity pages from source material
- `/research-prep` adds context for meeting subjects
- `/scan-intel` updates with new developments
- Graph auto-indexer connects entities across files
