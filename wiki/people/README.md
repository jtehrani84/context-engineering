# People Directory

Store structured knowledge about people you work with. One page per person.

## Why This Matters

After 30 days, you'll have 10-20 people pages. Before any meeting, Claude can load the relevant person's page and you'll have full context: their priorities, your history, their communication style, open threads.

## Getting Started

1. Start with 3-5 key people: your manager, your closest collaborator, your most important stakeholder
2. Use `_template-colleague.md` for internal contacts
3. Use `_template-external.md` for external contacts
4. Update pages after significant interactions (use /follow-up)

## How Pages Grow

- `/follow-up` adds interaction history
- `/research-prep` pulls in new context
- Manual notes from conversations
- Cron outputs that mention known people

## Tips

- Keep it factual, not judgy
- Update "Last interaction" after every significant exchange
- The "Pet peeves" field saves you from repeated mistakes
- "Go-to for" is the most useful field long-term
